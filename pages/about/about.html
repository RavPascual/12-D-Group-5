TECH UPGRADE

WHAT IS IT ABOUT?

This is a TRADE to UPGRADE CALCULATOR APPLICATION that can compute all the payables including the payables per month depending on what you will choose in the payment terms when you are planning to trade your phone to a new gadget.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

The application works by inputting all of the needed information such as the New Gadget Price, Old Phone Appraised Value and Payment Terms (9, 12 or 15 months only). After you input all the information needed, click the compute button. The app should give you all three outputs: Financed Amount, Toatal Interest Payable and Monthly Pament.
